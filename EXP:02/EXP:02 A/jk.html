Jammu and Kashmir
  • Jammu and Kashmir is a union territory in northern India, known for its stunning natural beauty, including the Himalayas, lush valleys, and serene lakes.
  • It is divided into two regions: Jammu, which is known for its temples, and Kashmir, famous for its picturesque landscapes and gardens.
  • The summer capital is Srinagar, known for its houseboats and the Dal Lake, while the winter capital is Jammu.
  • The region is also known for its rich cultural heritage, including traditional crafts, music, and cuisine.
  • Due to its strategic location, it has been a focal point of conflict, but it remains a popular destination for tourists and pilgrims.
