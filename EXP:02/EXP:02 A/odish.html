Odisha
  • Odisha, located on the eastern coast of India, is a state known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and natural beauty.
  • The capital city is Bhubaneswar, which is also known as the "City of Temples" due to its numerous ancient temples, including the famous Lingaraja Temple.
  • Odisha is home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Sun Temple at Konark, and the Jagannath Temple in Puri, which is a major pilgrimage site.
  • The state has a diverse landscape that includes the Eastern Ghats, fertile plains, and a coastline along the Bay of Bengal.
  • Odisha's economy is driven by agriculture, mining, and industrial sectors, particularly steel, aluminum, and power generation.
  • Odisha is also known for its vibrant festivals, traditional dance forms like Odissi, and unique art and crafts, including Pattachitra paintings and intricate silver filigree work.
  • The state's natural attractions include Chilika Lake, the largest coastal lagoon in India, and the Simlipal National Park, known for its biodiversity and tiger reserve.
